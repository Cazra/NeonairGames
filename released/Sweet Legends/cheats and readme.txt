                      Sweet Legends readme with cheats




controls: Select stuff on the title screen menu with the mouse.

       arrow keys move Rip around.
       shift makes Rip jump
       CTRL or control makes Rip attack
       


cheats: On the menu press enter to make a codes option pop up.
On the screen that it takes you two you can enter 1 of 2 cheats.

These are the cheats:

      drought      =      no rising water on flooding tower level
      youcheateryou       =          invincibility
